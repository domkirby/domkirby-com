I figured I would expand on my Purview Information Protection information by creating a general guide around information protection labels. In this guide, I take a deeper dive into Purview Information Protection and provide you with some implementation examples that can be applied to just about any small business (plus one hypothetical that is more advanced).

**This guide provides the following labeling scenarios:**

- Scenario 1: Let’s Just Mark What’s Confidential
- Scenario 2: Contractors or other non-employee personas
- Scenario 3: Departments
- Scenario 4: Pull out all the stops

The goal here is to provide some ideas that you can expand upon to get more clients classifying their data.
